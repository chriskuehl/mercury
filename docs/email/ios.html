Configuring with iOS

To configure your iOS device (iPhone, iPod Touch, iPad, etc.) to access Mercury Mail, follow the steps below:

  1. 1. Add Account

    In settings, go to "Mail, Contacts, and Calendars" and tap "Add Account".

  2. 2. Other

    Choose account type "Other".

  3. 3. Mail Account

    Choose "Add Mail Account".

  4. 4. Enter Information

    Enter your name, email, and password.

  5. 5. IMAP

    Make sure IMAP is selected at the top.

  6. 6. Incoming Mail Server

    Enter mail.mercury.techxonline.net into the host name, your full email address into "user name", and your password.

  7. 7. Outgoing Mail Server

    Enter the same information for outgoing mail server as for incoming mail server.

  8. 8. Verify Information

    Tap "Next" and wait for the information to verify.
